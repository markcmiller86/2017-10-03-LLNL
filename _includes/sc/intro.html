Software Carpentry aims to help researchers improve their productivity by teaching them basic and intermediate use of shell, git and python. This hands-on workshop will cover basic concepts and tools, including task automation, version control, data management, and secure, remote resource utilization. Participants will be encouraged to help one another and to apply what they have learned to their own research problems.

For more information on what we teach and why, please see our paper "Best Practices for Scientific Computing".
